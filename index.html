Order pickup

Scrap metal removal in 3 steps

INCOMING REQUEST

You call or leave a request on the site. Manager Nikolai gives a preliminary assessment of the scrap by phone based on a photo of your scrap (can be sent to any messenger or by mail)

Nicholas Presale manager

LOGISTICS

You confirm the time and place of pickup and the application is transferred to the logistics department. At the appointed time, we pick up the scrap and go with you to the collection point, where the receiver Ivan controls the weighing of the first loaded machine, then empty. The difference is your weight scrap.

Ivan Receiver

PAYMENT OF THE MONEY

You receive money for the metal immediately at the cash desk in cash or by bank transfer from the cashier Helena

Elena Cashier

How to understand how much do i have metal?

Submit a photo of the scrap metal. Within 5 minutes we will inform you of its approximate volume and cost
